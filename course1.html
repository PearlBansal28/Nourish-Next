Nutrition Education and Advocacy

Duration: 8 weeks

Target Audience:

Educators, health professionals, community leaders

Course Description:

This 8-week course provides comprehensive knowledge on nutrition science and public health nutrition. Participants will learn about the principles of healthy eating, nutritional needs across different life stages, and the impact of diet on health. The course also covers strategies for promoting healthy eating habits within communities, including advocacy techniques, communication skills, and community engagement practices.
