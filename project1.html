Rescue & Renew

Food Redistribution Network

"Saving Surplus, Feeding Communities"

Objective:

Reduce food waste by collecting surplus food from restaurants, grocery stores, and farms and redistributing it to those in need.

About the project:

This initiative aims to tackle food waste and hunger simultaneously by creating a robust network of volunteers who collect excess food from various sources such as restaurants, grocery stores, and farms. The collected food is then redistributed to local shelters, food banks, and community centers. The project also focuses on raising awareness about the importance of reducing food waste and the impact it can have on community well-being.
