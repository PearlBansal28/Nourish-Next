Community Cooking and Nutrition Workshops

Duration: 4 weeks

Target Audience:

Community health workers, volunteers

Course Description:

This 4-week course equips participants with the skills needed to organize and lead community cooking classes and nutrition workshops. The course focuses on creating affordable and nutritious recipes, teaching cooking techniques, and delivering engaging and informative workshops. Participants will also learn about food safety, budgeting for healthy meals, and ways to encourage community participation.
