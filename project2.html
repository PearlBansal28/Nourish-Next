BrightBites

School Meal Initiative

"Fueling Learning, One Meal at a Time"

Objective:

Ensure children have access to nutritious meals, enhancing their ability to learn and thrive.

About the project:

BrightBites focuses on providing free or subsidized nutritious meals to schoolchildren to support their academic and personal development. By partnering with local schools and sourcing food from nearby farms, the program ensures that children receive healthy, balanced meals. Additionally, the initiative integrates nutrition education into the school curriculum to teach children about healthy eating habits, thereby fostering long-term health and wellness.
